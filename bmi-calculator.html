Frequently Asked Questions

What do the BMI categories mean?
Under 18.5 is Underweight, 18.5–24.9 is Normal weight, 25–29.9 is Overweight, and 30+ is Obesity, using the standard WHO ranges.

Is BMI accurate for everyone?
BMI is a general screening measure and doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition — it isn't a diagnosis.

About the Ecommerce Product Editor

Ecommerce Product Editor is a real artboard-based editor — position, scale, and rotate your product photo as a layer, crop to any marketplace-friendly ratio, and use grid and smart guides to line everything up precisely. This is the foundation of a larger product-image workspace; this phase focuses on a genuinely professional core editing engine, with AI features and marketplace templates planned for later phases.

Features

  • Real artboard with the product photo as a positionable, scalable layer
  • Move, scale, rotate, and flip — with a live, pixel-identical preview
  • Professional crop tool with locked ratio presets (1:1, 4:5, 16:9, 9:16) or free crop
  • Rule-of-thirds and square grid overlays, plus center-snapping smart guides
  • Unlimited undo/redo history
  • Auto-save with session recovery if you refresh or close the tab by accident
  • Export as PNG (with transparency), JPG, or WEBP at full resolution

How to Use

Upload a product photo, then use Transform to position, scale, and rotate it on the artboard. Turn on grid or smart guides to line things up, use Crop for a specific marketplace ratio, and Export when you're ready — undo and redo are always available, and your session is saved automatically if you need to step away.

Benefits

  • Nothing is uploaded — the entire editor runs locally in your browser
  • No sign-up, no watermark, no premium lock — completely free
  • What you see in the editor is exactly what gets exported, pixel for pixel

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free, with no watermark?
Yes — this phase of the editor is completely free with no sign-up, no watermark, and no premium lock. Everything runs in your browser.

What image formats can I upload?
PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP, and AVIF. Export is offered as PNG, JPG, or WEBP — AVIF export isn't reliably supported across browsers yet, so it's not offered as an output format even though it's accepted as input.

Will my editing session be lost if I accidentally close the tab?
No — your session auto-saves in your browser as you work. Reopening the tool within 24 hours will offer to restore it.

Does this support text, shapes, or marketplace templates yet?
Not yet — this phase focuses on building a genuinely solid core editing engine (artboard, transform, crop, guides, history, export). Text, shapes, marketplace presets, and AI product features are planned for future phases.

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?
No — the entire editor, including the auto-save, runs locally in your browser. Your photo is never sent to a server.
